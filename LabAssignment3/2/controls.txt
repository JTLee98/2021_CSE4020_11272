glfw.KEY = transform
--------------------------------------------
W = Scale by 0.9 times in y dir
E = Scale by 1.1 times in y dir
S = Rotate by 10 degs CCW
D = Rotate by 10 degs CW
X = Translate by  0.1 in x dir 
C = Translate by -0.1 in x dir
R = Reflection across the origin
1 = Reset with ID matrix
